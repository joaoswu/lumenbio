Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is Lumenbio free?
Completely free during early access. We're capping it at 1,000 pages for now so everyone gets a fast, polished experience.
Do I need to know how to code?
Not at all. Everything is point-and-click from your dashboard, with a live preview that updates as you tweak.
What can I put on my page?
Live Discord presence, Last.fm now-playing, a music player, up to 10 social links, rotating locations, profile tags, a live clock and more — all themeable.
Can I change it later?
Anytime. Adjust colors, effects, integrations and links from your dashboard whenever you like — changes go live instantly.
What will my link look like?
Clean and shareable: lumenbio/u/yourname — one link for everything you are.
Is my account secure?
Yes. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text, and your session is kept in a secure, http-only cookie.
